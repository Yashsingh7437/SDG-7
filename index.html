Lets Learn about SDG 7

What is SDG7

SDG 7 aims to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all. This goal focuses on expanding infrastructure and upgrading technology to provide clean energy in all developing countries.

Why it Matters

Access to energy is crucial for the well-being of people and sustainable development. Clean and affordable energy can help to alleviate poverty, improve health, and combat climate change.

Take Action

Everyone can contribute to SDG 7. Here are some actions you can take:
Use energy-efficient appliances.

Support policies that promote renewable energy.

Raise awareness about the importance of clean energy.

Target 7.1

UNIVERSAL ACCESS TO MODERN ENERGY

By 2030, ensure universal access to affordable, reliable and modern energy services.

Target 7.2

INCREASE GLOBAL PERCENTAGE OF RENEWABLE ENERGY

By 2030, increase substantially the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix.

Target 7.3

DOUBLE THE IMPROVEMENT IN ENERGY EFFICIENCY

By 2030, double the global rate of improvement in energy efficiency.

Target 7.4

PROMOTE ACCESS TO RESEARCH, TECHNOLOGY AND INVESTMENTS IN CLEAN ENERGY

By 2030, enhance international cooperation to facilitate access to clean energy research and technology, including renewable energy, energy efficiency and advanced and cleaner fossil-fuel technology, and promote investment in energy infrastructure and clean energy technology.

Target 7.5

EXPAND AND UPGRADE ENERGY SERVICES FOR DEVELOPING COUNTRIES

By 2030, expand infrastructure and upgrade technology for supplying modern and sustainable energy services for all in developing countries, in particular least developed countries, small island developing States and landlocked developing countries, in accordance with their respective programmes of support.
